Step 1: Import volumes Import your volume file into the web portal by clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import Volumes box.



Once uploaded, press "Execute"!


Step 2: Import particles Import your particles files into the web portal by clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import particle box.



Once uploaded, press "Execute"!


Step 3: Validate Select the validation box validate overfitting.



Parameters can be easily customized. Two level of usages are supported (Normal and Advanced), which should be chosen according to your experience.



Finally press "Execute"!


Method's results can be further analyzed by first clicking on the corresponding Method box and then clicking on the Analyze Results button.
